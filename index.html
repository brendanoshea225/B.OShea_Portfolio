About Me

I am a freelance web developer and frontend engineer based in Brooklyn, New York. I am passionate about designing and deploying websites that cater to my clients' vision and branding.

My love for design and expertise in frontend technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React enable me to bring visions to life with precision. From crafting responsive websites to developing intuitive user interfaces, I thrive on delivering clean code and seamless functionality that exceeds expectations. Let's build something amazing together!

Services

Design

My process begins with the initial design of the site or app. I work closely with the client to ensure their branding expectations and vision are being met. I adhere to proven design standards while allowing room for creative freedom.

Development

The development stage is undertaken with exact precision to guarantee that all guidelines, accessibility, and best practices are put to use. Collaboration between other developers is encouraged - let's work together!

Deployment

The project is ready to be deployed online upon the client's final approval of its design and development! I navigate the deployment process and ensure that the customer is as prepared as possible to have the project in their own hands.
